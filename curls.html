The Four Houses

Find your house.

We sort curls into four houses — Wave, Spiral, Coil, and Cloud. Each has its own appetite, its own personality, its own ritual.

3a

House of the Wave

Loose, cinematic spirals the size of a marker. Hair that lives in the wind — gorgeous, but easily flattened by heavy product. Your curls want featherweight.

Recommended ritual · Coconut Cloud Leave-In + Flaxseed Pageantry
3b

House of the Spiral

Springy ringlets, pencil-width, full of attitude. Drinks more than 3a, holds a curl like a dream. Your hair likes a creamy mid-weight styler that lets the spring breathe.

Recommended ritual · Hibiscus Wash + Sun-Drunk Elixir
3c · 4a

House of the Coil

Tight, defined corkscrews — the most photogenic shrinkage in the game. Hair that thrives on hydration layers: water, leave-in, cream, gel — a four-course wash day.

Recommended ritual · Mango Mask + Midnight Butter
4b · 4c

House of the Cloud

Soft halos, zig-zag patterns, undeniable presence. Hair that asks for the deepest hydration the bottle can give — and rewards you with shape, density, and quiet luxury.

Recommended ritual · Mango Velvet Mask + Edges & Audacity
The Ritual

A Sunday wash in four movements.

I.

Soak.

Hibiscus Wash Day, scalp-first. Massage in slow circles. Let your shoulders drop.

II.

Drench.

Mango Velvet Mask, root to tip. Section in four. Pin up. Twenty minutes minimum. Pour yourself something cold.

III.

Layer.

Soaking wet, layer Coconut Cloud Leave-In, then Midnight Curl Butter. Praying hands. Squish to condish.

IV.

Crown.

Plop in cotton tee for 20 minutes. Diffuse on low. A drop of Sun-Drunk Elixir to seal. Catch a glance at the mirror. Smile. Reign.

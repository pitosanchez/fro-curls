The Philosophy

A house built on four promises.

Every formula begins on the islands — ingredients pulled from the same earth that grew us. Clean. Conscious. Conjured for the way our hair actually moves.

01 / Botanicals

Island ingredients.

Coconut, hibiscus, cassava, mango butter — sourced from the Caribbean cooperatives that raised us.

02 / Science

Curl-true chemistry.

Cosmetic chemistry tuned for 3a–4c coils. No surfactants that strip, no silicones that lie.

03 / Patience

Slow beauty.

Small batches, hand-poured. Because curls deserve the patience of a perfumer.

04 / Honour

Unmistakably us.

Vegan. Cruelty-free. Designed by women whose hair has lived all your hair has lived.

Volume 01 · Hydrate
The Ritual

A weekly soak.
A standing date with your crown.

Curls are thirsty things. They drink slow. The FroCurls weekly mask is built for the kind of hydration that lives inside the strand for days — not the kind that rinses out by dinner.

  • Cold-pressed coconut + avocado butter for deep slip
  • Hibiscus extract to lock the cuticle and shine the surface
  • Built-in detangler — finger-comb dreaming

About FroCurls

Meet Crystal aka FRO Curls

Crystal's curly hair journey began in 2015, when she made the decision to embrace her natural curls and embark on a path to healthier hair. Ever since, she's been dreaming of creating a brand that celebrates and supports the beauty of natural hair.

With a passion for self-love and curly hair empowerment, Crystal founded FRO Curls with the mission of providing high-quality, natural hair care products designed specifically for curly hair. At FRO Curls, we believe that every curl is unique, and our products are tailored to nourish and enhance the natural beauty of your curls.

Meet Crystal

Curls were never the problem.

"I spent twenty years apologizing for hair that didn't need an apology. FroCurls is the love letter I owed her."

Crystal's curl journey began in 2015, in a Brooklyn bathroom with a pair of kitchen scissors and a YouTube tutorial playing on her phone. The big chop wasn't a beauty decision — it was a homecoming. Her hair, the way her grandmother in Santo Domingo wore hers. The way her cousins on the island still do.

What she found wasn't on the shelves. Not really. Products built for "ethnic hair" that left her coils crispy. Formulas full of silicone shortcuts. Brands that talked to her like she was a problem to solve, rather than a crown to dress.

So she built her own. Six years of formulating in the kitchen with her aunties. Trips back to the Dominican Republic to study the salons that had quietly perfected curl care for generations. A textbook on cosmetic chemistry. A lot of bad batches. A few magic ones.

FroCurls is what came out of that. A house, not a brand. A ritual, not a routine. A curl atelier built by, and for, the bold Caribbean crowns of the world.

— Crystal
The Values

Three things we refuse to compromise.

01 / Honesty

Honest formulas.

If it doesn't earn its place in the bottle, it doesn't get in. No fillers. No silicone smoke-and-mirrors. No language we couldn't explain to your auntie.

02 / Roots

Caribbean sourcing.

Hibiscus from Dominican cooperatives. Coconut from Jamaican farms. We pay above-market, in dollars, direct. The economy that grew us, we grow back.

03 / Truth

Curl-true testing.

Every formula is tested on real women with real coils — across 3a, 3b, 3c, 4a, 4b, 4c. Tested by them, not on them. Approved by their crowns.

FroCurls is what happens when a Caribbean girl stops asking permission from the shelf.

The Atelier

Hand-poured.
Small batch. Slow on purpose.

Every bottle is mixed and poured in our Brooklyn studio. Small batches mean fresh formulas — not stockroom forgotten. It also means we know exactly which lot your bottle came from, who poured it, and what the humidity was that day.

It's the way good rum is made. The way good perfume is made. We didn't see a reason to do hair differently.
